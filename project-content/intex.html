IntEx

I built this app to help my friend find a Turkish perfume she had bought on her last trip to Turkey.
IntEx (international exchange) is a listings platform that helps users procure goods that exist in other countries but are not sold in their own. Simply post a listing and get connected with people in that country.

Built with HTML/CSS/JS/BOOTSTRAP/PYTHON/FLASK/POSTGRESQL
Supported functionalities:

  • Facebook Login
  • Post Listings
  • Edit Your Listings
  • Delete Your Listings
  • See All Posted Listings By Country
